WELCOME TO FPI ALUMNI

WELCOME ADDRESS

Mr. YUSUFF, Jamiu Adebisi

President

Dear Friends and Fellow Alumni, it is my pleasure to welcome you all to this site; the newly-launched page of the Alumni Association on the website of our Alma Mater – The Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro.

This marks another milestone in the efforts of the School Management ably led by the Rector, Architect Olusegun Aluko, and the current Alumni Exco at giving the Association a solid foundation and a befitting image/brand.

This website is meant to provide members, intending members, our friends and the general public a seamless and very convenient channel for interacting with the Association. The site will provide news, updates, educational information, and showcase our various projects.

Once again, I welcome you to our page. Please relax and enjoy your ‘tour’ of the page.

EXECUTIVES

The current president of the federal Polythechnic Ilaro Alumni Association is Mr. Yusuff, Jamiu Adebisi. He leads the association, which was relaunched to provide a solid foundation and better image, offering a seamless channel for alumni interaction through the official alumni page on the polytechnic's website.

Key aspects of his role and the Alumni Association:

Leadership:

Mr. Yusuff presides over the Alumni Executive committee, which was formed to give the association a strong foundation and a unified brand.

Alumni Engagement:

He works to foster interaction among members and the general public through the association's new website.

Association History:

The association was revived by eminent personalities from the Lagos and llaro chapters and was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, with functioning chapters in various locations like Lagos, the UK, Abuja, and Abeokuta. ©
The current General Secretary of the Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro Alumni Association is Mr. Adeyemi, Adebiyi Bankole. He serves on the Executive Committee of the association, which was launched on the institution's website to facilitate member interaction and provide updates on the association's activities.

More details:

Mr. Adeyemi, Adebiyi Bankole is listed as part of the current Executive Committee, according to the association's website.
This Alumni Association's website serves as a platform for news, updates, and information regarding the activities of the association and the Polytechnic itself.
The website also provides details on membership procedures and showcases the association's various projects, including the completion of a hostel for male students on campus.
