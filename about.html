ABOUT THE
FPI ALUMNI

About Us

The Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro Alumni Association (FPI Alumni) is the umbrella body of all graduates of Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro since its establishment in 1979. Over the years, the institution has produced thousands of graduates who today make up a vibrant alumni community spread across Nigeria and in different parts of the world. These alumni have become engineers, scientists, entrepreneurs, academics, civil servants, business executives, politicians, and innovators contributing greatly to the development of society.

Purpose and Mission of the Alumni

The Alumni Association was established to foster unity among graduates and to build a strong relationship between the Polytechnic and its former students. Its key purposes include:

  • Networking and Unity: creating a platform for old students to stay connected, share opportunities, and support one another.
  • Support for the Institution: contributing financially and materially towards the growth and development of the Polytechnic.
  • Student Development: mentoring current students, offering scholarships, and providing career guidance.
  • Promoting FPI's Image: projecting Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro as one of Nigeria's foremost polytechnics.
  • National Development: using the skills and positions of members to positively influence society and the nation's technological growth.

Activities and Contributions

The Alumni Association is not just symbolic; it is actively involved in many ways:

  • Reunions and Homecoming: Alumni gather at intervals for reunions and annual general meetings, strengthening bonds among graduates.
  • Infrastructure Development: The association has supported the institution by donating ICT equipment, laboratory tools, lecture materials, and even contributing to the renovation of facilities.
  • Scholarship Programs: Some alumni chapters sponsor scholarships and financial aid for brilliant but indigent students.
  • Professional Development: Alumni organize seminars, workshops, and public lectures that benefit both students and the wider community.
  • Mentorship: Through structured mentorship, senior alumni guide younger graduates and current students on career paths and entrepreneurship.

Structure and Organization

The FPI Alumni Association is structured with national, state, and international chapters, ensuring that graduates across the globe can actively participate. Each chapter has an executive council, usually made up of a President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Public Relations Officer, and other officers.
The National Alumni Secretariat in Ilaro serves as the coordinating center, linking the chapters together and ensuring unity of purpose. Chapters are active in states such as Lagos, Ogun, Abuja, Rivers, and even outside Nigeria in countries where many graduates reside.

Achievements of FPI Alumni

Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro Alumni are known for excellence in different fields. They occupy notable positions in government, private sector, academia, and entrepreneurship. Many are successful business owners contributing to Nigeria's economy by creating jobs. Some achievements of the alumni body include:

  • Sponsorship of ICT development projects within the Polytechnic.
  • Organization of career fairs to prepare students for life after graduation.
  • Financial support for indigent students and sponsorship of skill-acquisition programs.
  • Strengthening the institution's reputation as one of the most disciplined and respected polytechnics in Nigeria.

Conclusion

The Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro Alumni Association represents the strength, success, and unity of graduates of the institution. It is more than just a body of old students; it is a global family of professionals committed to uplifting their alma mater, supporting current students, and contributing to the technological and economic development of Nigeria. By sustaining close ties with the Polytechnic, the alumni continue to uphold the institution's motto: "Technology for Development.""
